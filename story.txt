A kid named Chadwick grew up on the mean streets of Hurley, WI.  He was a street urchin from birth, never knowing where or when his next meal would be.  He slept under trees and benches.  But, when he turned 12 years old, he discovered he had a magical power.

Suddenly, his parents are kidnaped by evil flying unicorns. With his almighty magical power, Chadwick must save his parents. His adventure has just began.
